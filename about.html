About Us

Home About Us

Positra Packaging Pvt. Ltd. registered with a vision to establish as one of the best prominent supplier and exporter of packaging products.

This venture has been initiated by Plastic professionals having three decade of working experience in the manufacturing and global marketing of complete packaging products from India.

Positra Packaging has conducted strict factory audit of numerous manufacturing facilities and finally decided to work with only few producers whom not only have good physical infrastructure setup but also good quality management systems and above all good mind set to serve International clientle.

Positra Packaging has entered into strategic alliance with short listed producers and doing contract maufacturing depending on the product requirements. We are in a position to offer quality product at competitive prices with prompt delivery.
As promoters of the company are having good experience about packaging industry, hence focus will be on pre and post sales service with an objective to offer innovative solution to customers packaging requirement.

While others assure good quality and prices but we ensure that it happens and customer goes through pleasant buying experience.
