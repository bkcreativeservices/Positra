SILO BAGS

Silo bags are made of a triple layer of thick laminated extruded plastic (polyethylene) about 230-235 microns thick; the two outer layers are white with UV protective coating while the inner layer in contact with the grain is black. When fully loaded with grain, they are a long sausage-like tubular shape of about 8 to 12 ft in diameter and up to 200 ft in length and can hold 8,000 to 12,000 bushels of grain.

They have been used for storing commodities for human on farms in Argentina, Australia, Canada and the U.S. Commodities stored include corn, soybeans, wheat, barley and sunflower.

Advantages and Benefits of silo Bags
1. Eliminates costly on farm storage systems
2. Very low cost per ton of stored grain
3. The on farm storage of your grains greatly expands the marketing opportunities of the commodity
4. Grains can be stored at a higher moisture content than is allowed in traditional receival systems
5. Store on farm with no wastage or slippage
6. Very low cost per tonne of stored grain
7. Storage in a year of high/excess production is unlimited
8. You only buy what storages you need
9. The system is totally portable/relocatable
10. Grains can be stored as to variety, grade, protein or for any other reason to segregate
11. The segregated grains can be easily sampled by means of a conventional sampler
12. Grain retains its quality and colour
13. The bag is airtight, therefore chemicals are not normally required to control insects
14. Perfect for organic commodities
15. Can eliminate costly harvester downtime
16. Perfect for organic commodities
17. Very quick implementation of large tonnage sites
18. Suitable for both extreme heat and extreme cold climates
