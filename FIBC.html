FIBC / JUMBO BAGS / BULK BAGS / BIG BAGS

Positra Packaging Pvt. Ltd. offers you FIBC / Bulk bags / Jumbo bags / Big bags in various designs like Panel, Circular & Baffle. Our polypropylene big bags are available in different shapes and sizes which can be customized depending upon customer usage.

Big Bags are volume bags made out of UV stabilized polypropylene: exceedingly strong bags which will be used for the storage and transportation of heavy materials and products. These bags can be useful for various industries such as chemicals, dyes-intermediates, pharmaceuticals, minerals, rubber, sugar etc.
